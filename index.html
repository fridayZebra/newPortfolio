About Me

My name is Firoz (fee-rose) and I'm a Frontend Engineer out of Anaheim, CA. I wanted to build applications that weren't being built, solve problems that weren't being solved and be challenged (and frustrated) every day. So, I left my life in investment finance and found a new home in web development.

I haven't been doing this very long, but can already say that it's the best decision I've ever made. I LOVE making things efficient- from simple things like teaching people keyboard short cuts to automating processes.
